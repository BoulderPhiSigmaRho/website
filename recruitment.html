Spring 2018 Recruitment

How do I join?

Phi Sigma Rho has a relaxed recruitment environment. If you are intersted in joining, come to any of our recruitment events, or contact us directly if you are unable to attend. Our mocktail event is our only event with a dress code, and we ask that you dress nicely as it is more formal.
