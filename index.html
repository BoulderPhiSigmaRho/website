Together we build the future.

Phi Sigma Rho is a social sorority for women in STEM majors.

Friendship

We foster a unique community for women in STEM through social events and activities.

Scholarship

We encourage academic excellence through group studying and promote well-rounded students through philanthropy in the community.

Encouragement

We strive to build up our sisters in all of their endeavors, whether professional or personal.

About Us

Valentine's Social

We hold a variety of social events throughout the semester. We celebrated Valentine's Day by exchanging valentines, making Valentine-themed crafts, and eating lots of sweets.

Epsilon Class Induction

We recently inducted our Epsilon Class of six lovely ladies: Kyla, Claire, Kirsten, Jehan, Kat, and Haley. Click here to learn more about becoming part of out Zeta Class this fall!

We adopted a penguin!

Since penguins are the sorority mascot, this year we symbolically adopted a penguin through WWF.

Formal with Theta Tau

The last two years, we have been invited to Theta Tau's formal. Here we are at the Rayback Collective for the most recent formal.

Bake sale

We believe philanthropy is an important part of being a well-rounded individual and have held a number of bake sales for charities.

Study help

While we focus on providing a social balance to the stresses of college, we often study together or help each other with schoolwork.

Installed Jan. 23, 2017

We were installed as the Alpha Mu chapter of Phi Sigma Rho on February 23rd, 2017. The Alpha and Beta classes of our chapter were initiated, and we began our journey together as sisters.

Delta Class Initiation

We recently initiated our Delta Class of four lovely ladies: Jade, Lauren, Maddy, and Musqan. Click here to learn more about becoming part of out Epsilon Class!

Retreat

Once a year, we hold a sorority retreat to get away from the stress of school. This year we went up to Estes Park.
