Alumni

Once a sister, always a sister.

Phi Sigma Rho is a sisterhood for life. Even after graduation, sisters keep in touch and are always invited to come back and take part sorority social events and rituals. Every woman leaves a beautiful, everlasting mark on the sorority after she leaves. Here is where we acknowledge those ladies who helped us grow but are now forging their own path, whether in graduate education, industry, family, or something else.
